WoodSnap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Overview

WoodSnap is developed by Chad Newbry LLC. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect (spoiler: none) and how the App works.

Data Collection

WoodSnap does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No photos leave your device. All wood identification is performed on-device using Core ML.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are included.
  • No account creation is required.
  • No personal information is collected.

Camera Access

WoodSnap requests camera access solely to photograph wood surfaces for identification. Photos are processed locally and are never uploaded to any server.

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Manage subscriptions in your Apple ID settings.

Third-Party Services

WoodSnap does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising frameworks.

Children's Privacy

WoodSnap does not collect data from any user, including children. The App is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at support@chadnewbry.com.
