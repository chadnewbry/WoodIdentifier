Frequently Asked Questions

How does wood identification work?

WoodSnap uses an on-device AI model to analyze wood grain patterns, color, and texture from your camera. No internet connection is required.

How accurate is the identification?

Accuracy depends on lighting, wood condition, and photo quality. For best results, photograph a clean, well-lit surface showing clear grain patterns.

How do I manage my subscription?

Open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → WoodSnap Pro. You can change or cancel anytime.

Is my data private?

Yes. WoodSnap collects zero data. See our Privacy Policy.
